## IP Address
IP adress is a unique ID for each device to connect over a internet

IP address represent as 4 bytes, example: 128.64.32.16 

1 byte = 8 bit 

## 8bit

### 192.64.32.16

192 how to convert this into binary form
To convert 192 into binary form, you break it down into powers of 2: {DO LCM for 192}

Now read the remainders backwards: 11000000

### So, the binary representation of 192 is: 11000000
